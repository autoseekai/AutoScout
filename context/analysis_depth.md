# Analysis Depth Guidelines

The **Deep Analyst** and **Insight Synthesizer** should use these definitions to calibrate their output.

## Level 1: Surface (Scanning)
- Purpose: What happened?
- Scope: Title, summary, and primary entities involved.

## Level 2: Technical (Deep Dive)
- Purpose: How does it work?
- Scope: Implementation details, architectural trade-offs, step-by-step logic, or mathematical foundations.

## Level 3: Strategic (Synthesis)
- Purpose: Why does it matter to the user's specific long-term goals?
- Scope: Competitive landscape changes, future projections, and alignment with the user's stored "Interest Profile".

## Triggering Levels
- **Level 1**: Default for all scanned content.
- **Level 2**: Triggered when a piece of content exceeds a Signal Score of 4/5.
- **Level 3**: Triggered specifically during the **Weekly Synthesize** stage or on explicit user request.
