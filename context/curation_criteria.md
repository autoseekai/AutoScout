# Content Curation & Signal Selection Criteria

All content found by the **Web Scouts** must be evaluated against these standards.

## 1. Signal-to-Noise Ratio (SNR)
- **High Signal**: Peer-reviewed papers, detailed engineering blogs, primary source interviews, raw financial data.
- **Low Signal**: "Top 10" lists, non-technical hype, repetitive news, promotional content.

## 2. Evaluation Matrix (1-5 Scale)
- **Technical Depth**: Does the content provide "how-to" or "why" rather than just "what"?
- **Uniqueness**: Is this information rare or a common aggregate?
- **Actionability**: Can the user make a decision based on this?

## 3. Hard Rejection Criteria
- Any content behind a hard paywall.
- Content that is purely AI-generated without human editorial value.
- Short-form social media noise without linked long-form evidence.
