# Daily Digest Output Specification

The **Note Keeper** must format the daily report as follows:

## Structure
1. **Header**: Date and high-level mood (Optimistic, Cautious, Volatile).
2. **Executive Summary**: 3 bullet points summarizing the most critical shifts in tracked interests.
3. **Category Deep Dives**:
   - **[Category Name]**
   - *Key Insight*: 1-2 sentences.
   - *Source Links*: Bulleted list of curated high-signal URLs.
   - *So What?*: Analysis of why this matters to the user.
4. **The "Alpha" of the Day**: The single most important piece of information found across all categories.
5. **Next Steps**: Recommended follow-up research or reading.

## Styling
- Use bold text for key terms.
- Use blockquotes for critical warnings or quotes from sources.
- Keep total length under 1000 words.
